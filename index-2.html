A little bit about me —

Hello, I'm Clara, I started my career in the world of communication, where I specialized in my main passions: art,technology and the creation of products that improve people's lives, always with a focus on sustainable innovation.

I currently work as a technology consultant at Atrevia and as an advisor and curator in different projects related to emerging art and NFT. My previous experiences include working as an editor at the Web3 magazine W3volution, as an assistant in the department of Ancient and Modern painting at the Thyssen Bornemisza Collection Foundation, as Project Manager & NFT Advisor at Photential Art, as Communications Director of the territorial museum "Campos del Renacimiento" at Puentia and at We Collect as Gallery Asistant.

I have a degree in Journalism and Humanities from Carlos III de Madrid and I have different specialization courses in blockchain and contemporary and emerging art. I believe in the power of design in technology, helping to create a fluid interaction and an enjoyable experience between humans and products, pragmatically solving complex problems with tactical solutions and bringing a conceptual idea to reality.

Get in touch with me! I would love to chat about work, projects, design, art, technology or whatever:) oh and of course, May the AI be with you!
